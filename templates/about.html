About

Hi! I’m Felicia Mann, 10+ years in Software Engineering, front-end focused, back-end learner, full-stack supporter. I love what I do! Such a great mix of constantly learning new technologies, tools and coding while also getting to know, learn from and work with smart problem-solvers every day.

I’m a lover of the outdoors, working-out and hanging with my kiddos. I try to combine those three things as much as possible as a busy mother of two (and 2 doggos), time is of the essence.

Depending on the season you can usually find me snowboarding, hiking, camping, paddleboarding, working on the yard, at the park, trampoline jumpin’, talking to my plants or playing pretend with the kids. I love making a good home-cooked meal, cozy movie nights, a good book and spending time outdoors. I am from Albuquerque, New Mexico and have lived in Denver and surrounding areas for over a decade. I love to meet new people and grow my network so please reach out if you'd like to connect.
